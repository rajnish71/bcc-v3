Real Database, Real Edge Cases

Generated from live portal data, the card must never break. Missing photographs, very long names and absent contact fields all resolve to deliberate states.

Photo present
Portrait fills a 4:5-ish frame via object-fit:cover with focal-point bias toward the upper third.
Photo missing
Elegant fallback: warm plate, aperture monogram at 22% opacity, PHOTO PENDING label. Never a broken image.
Long name + long ID
Name auto-steps 22→19→17px; membership number is mono and ellipsis-clamped on a single line.

Recommended Direction

Ivory editorial credential with a single aperture accent.

Warm ivory (not corporate white, not government blue) and an ink footer give the card museum-membership gravity. The colourful aperture mark — BCC's strongest equity — does the brand work, while a single category accent keeps seven tiers legible and grayscale-safe. Square photo frames echo darkroom prints; mono data reads as a precise instrument. It is unmistakably a photography society's credential, yet trivial to generate from the database and faithful across PDF, PVC, mobile and home print.

WHY IT FITS BCC
Photography-first, community-warm, credible. Reads as a respected society, never a workplace badge.
WHY IT SCALES
One template, seven accents, infinite members. New tiers are a colour token, not a redesign.
WHY IT LASTS
Hierarchy is type + hairlines, not effects. Ages over a 5–10 year horizon and survives grayscale.
