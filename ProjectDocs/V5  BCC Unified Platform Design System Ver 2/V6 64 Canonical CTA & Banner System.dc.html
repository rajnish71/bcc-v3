Token Contract

CTA-ARCH-001 — Governing Constraints

Inviolable Rules

—One Primary CTA maximum per component. Never two gold gradient buttons.
—No gradients on panel backgrounds. Gold gradient reserved for Primary CTA button only.
—No glassmorphism, no neumorphism, no heavy shadows outside --e-cta.
—Gold hairline top-edge on all dark surface panels (--hairline-gold).
—No new button styles. No colour outside V6 98 token.css palette.
—Image placeholders use natural ratio — no object-fit:cover, no border-radius.

Surface Assignments

Dark--dark-100 · #141210 — Editorial CTA, Full Width, Event, Newsletter, Announcement (B)
Ivory--surface-0 · #FFFFFF — Editorial CTA (B), Split CTA, Inline CTA, Feature, Sponsor
