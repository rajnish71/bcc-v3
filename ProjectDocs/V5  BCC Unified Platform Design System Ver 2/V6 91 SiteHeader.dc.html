Behaviour & Animation

Behaviour Detail Timing / Easing GSAP Hook
Scroll condense After 60px scroll: height shrinks, surface darkens, logo inverts. All properties transition simultaneously. 0.35s · power3.out header-condense
Dropdown open Fade + 8px vertical slide from trigger point. Closes on outside click, Escape, or nav-away. 0.18s · power2.out —
Avatar menu open Same as dropdown. Right-aligned. Closes on outside click or Escape. 0.18s · power2.out —
Drawer open Full-screen overlay slides in from right. Backdrop dims to 40% black. Body scroll locked while open. 0.28s open · 0.22s close —
Nav hover Item transitions to navActiveColor. Underline does not extend on hover — it is reserved for the current route only. 0.15s · ease —
prefers-reduced-motion All GSAP transforms disabled. Colour and opacity changes remain — they are not vestibular triggers. immediate · no easing —
09

Accessibility & Responsive

Accessibility Requirements
Role: role="banner" on <header>
Nav: role="navigation" aria-label="Main"
Hamburger: aria-label="Open menu" aria-expanded
Dropdowns: aria-haspopup aria-expanded
Avatar menu: aria-label="Account menu"
Touch targets: minimum 44×44px on all interactive elements
Focus trap: keyboard focus trapped inside open drawer
Skip link: "Skip to main content" first focusable element
Current page: aria-current="page" on active nav link
Coming Soon: aria-disabled="true" + visually hidden label
Responsive Breakpoints
Breakpoint Behaviour
>900px Full desktop nav. All items visible. Dropdowns on hover.
600–900px Hamburger drawer. Logo 28px. CTA chips only in header.
<600px Same as 600–900px. Safe area insets via env(safe-area-inset-*).
Tablet Follows 600–900px rules. Landscape may show partial desktop nav.
Sticky behaviour: Header is always sticky. No hide-on-scroll — the nav is always accessible. The condensed surface is the "scrolled" affordance.
Safe areas: Padding respects env(safe-area-inset-top) on iPhone notch/Dynamic Island. Drawer uses env(safe-area-inset-bottom) for bottom padding.
