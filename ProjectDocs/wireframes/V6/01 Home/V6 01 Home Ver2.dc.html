CMS Implementation Note — Hero Spotlight Image Selection
The Hero Spotlight image is administrator-selectable from the Admin Dashboard. The CMS must allow administrators to browse and select only photographs having a 16:9 aspect ratio. To support this efficiently, the uploaded photo record must store the dimensions (width and height) of the uploaded image; the system derives the aspect ratio from these stored dimensions. These dimensions must be extracted from the FINAL uploaded image — not from the photographer's original RAW file. Photographers frequently crop and recompose images before uploading. The uploaded image represents the final artistic composition and is therefore the version that determines aspect ratio eligibility.

About Bhopal Camera Club

Photography is our discipline.
Bhopal is our subject.

Bhopal Camera Club was founded in 2016 by a group of photographers who believed a camera club should do more than host contests. We wanted to build a genuine learning community — one where a beginner could walk beside an experienced photographer, where critique happened with generosity, and where the practice of photography was valued as much as any prize.

Today, more than 340 members explore Bhopal's remarkable visual world together — its forts and lakes, wildlife corridors and monsoon skies. We organise monthly photowalks, host workshops with nationally recognised photographers, and exhibit member work at the city's cultural institutions. We are affiliated members of the Federation of Indian Photography, the only camera club in Central India with this recognition.
