Hero Copy — SEO Editorial Introduction

The Journal is not a blog. It is the editorial publication of Bhopal Camera Club — a record of what we see, where we go, and what we find worth documenting. It exists to preserve the photographic practice of our members: the planning behind a dawn shoot at the Upper Lake, the patience required to photograph a Painted Stork colony in winter, the history of a haveli that will not exist in ten years. You will find photo essays here, field reports from wildlife corridors and heritage sites, accounts of club expeditions to the Chambal, the Pench and the Western Ghats, and essays on the nature of light, the ethics of street photography, and the relationship between a photographer and a place photographed over twenty years. Every essay that appears in this publication becomes part of the permanent record of Bhopal Camera Club. We have been photographing this city since 1965. This is what we have seen.

~175 words · SEO-friendly · crawlable introductory copy · no CTA

Context-Sensitive Logic
When ALL is active: server returns up to 4 articles with featured:true across all categories, sorted by publication date. When a category is selected: server returns up to 4 featured articles in that category. When zero: section is absent from DOM — no placeholder, no empty state, no spacing gap.
